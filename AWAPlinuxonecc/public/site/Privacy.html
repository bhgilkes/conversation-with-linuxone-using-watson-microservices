Cognitive Systems

Cognitive systems are emerging as a System of Engagement They are providing the ability to automate human interaction and emulate reasoning for the benefit of the customer.

Trend & Motivation Cognitive systems can transform how organizations, think, act and operate in the future by learning through interactions and then providing evidence-based response guidance that can lead the organization toward better outcomes.

Relevant Use Case Many Cognitive capabilities are in use daily through Digital Personal Assistants, and are emerging as a key Customer Service resource for healthcare, insurance and other organizations.

Implications

  • Language becomes the user interface with Cognitive systems, through their ability to interpret human speech in a vocal or text based format.
  • Cognitive systems provide the ability to generate hypothesis and apply reasoning to situational context, through analytic algorithms and decision models.
  • Cognitive systems learn from past experiences, adjusting and recalibrating their responses based on prior interactions.

Takeaway Thought Cognitive systems can enhance self-service via text based identification and clarification of a customer’s problem and then transitioning the call to Customer Service representative, to focus on assisting the customer.

Recommendation Engage the Watson Developer Cloud Program where technology and cognitive experts are available to help envision enhancements to existing services capabilities (e.g., Customer Care: Self Service, Agent Assistance, Virtual Agent).
